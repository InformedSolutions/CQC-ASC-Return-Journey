Do you employ any full time equivalent posts?

What is classified as full time?

Use 37 hours a week as your basis for full time, even if your full time hours are different

How to calculate full time equivalent posts

Full time equivalent is a way of setting out the number of hours people work in a given period. It divides the total number of staff hours by the hours for one full time employee. To calculate it:

  • add together the hours worked by all your staff in one week
  • divide the total by 37

Who undertakes regular activities?

Which staff to include:

  • Give the number of people who provide care we regulate as part of their day to day job, for example care workers and nurses.
  • Do not include people who only do things we do not regulate, like cooking or cleaning.
  • Count permanent, temporary, pool and agency staff, students and volunteers.
  • Include staff on zero hour contracts.
  • Do not include vacancies.
