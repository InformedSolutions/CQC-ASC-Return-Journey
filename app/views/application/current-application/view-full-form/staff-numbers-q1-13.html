How to calculate full time equivalent posts
Full time equivalent is a way of setting out the number of hours people work in a given period. It divides the total number of staff hours by the hours for one full time employee. To calculate it:
  • add together the hours worked by all your staff in one week
  • divide the total by 37
Which staff to include
  • Give the number of people who provide care we regulate as part of their day to day job
  • Do not include people who do things we do not regulate, like cooking or cleaning
  • Count permanent, temportary, pool, agency staff, students and volunteers
  • Also include staff on zero hour contracts
  • Do not include vacancies
